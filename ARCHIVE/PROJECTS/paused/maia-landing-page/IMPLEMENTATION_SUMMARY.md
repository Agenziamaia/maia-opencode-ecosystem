## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **maia-primary**: #FF6B35 (Orange)
- **maia-secondary**: #004E89 (Blue)
- **maia-accent**: #F7C59F (Cream)
- **maia-dark**: #1A1A2E (Dark Blue)
- **maia-light**: #F0F4F8 (Light Blue)

### Design Principles
1. **Glassmorphism**: Semi-transparent cards with backdrop blur
2. **Gradient Gradients**: Rich gradients for buttons and backgrounds
3. **Micro-interactions**: Hover effects, scale animations
4. **Spatial Composition**: Generous spacing, asymmetrical layouts
5. **Visual Hierarchy**: Clear typography, color-coded priorities
